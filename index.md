## public projects

**[SREG](https://github.com/lucaspecina/synthetic-research-envs)** — Synthetic Research Environment Generator
Generates synthetic research cases backed by structural causal models. The agent investigates freely. Every answer is scored against formal ground truth, not LLM judges. Built for evaluation and RL training of scientific reasoning.

**AI-Driven Chemical Formulation** — Y-TEC
Agent system for chemical formulation and stabilization. Closed-loop with a real wet lab: AI proposes, lab validates, results feed back.

**[Grubrics](https://github.com/lucaspecina/grubrics-science)**
RL-trained rubric generation with Spearman correlation reward. Exploring application as a scientific claim evaluator.

**[Research Replay](https://github.com/lucaspecina/research-replay)**
Synthetic replay of published research trajectories. Can an AI reconstruct a discovery path with and without hindsight?
